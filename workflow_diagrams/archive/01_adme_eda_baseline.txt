═══════════════════════ PHASES 4–6 (PLANNED) ═════════════════════════════════

 Phase 4: Learning curves — vary fraction ∈ {0.05, 0.10, 0.25, 0.50, 0.75, 1.0}
          Two arms: baseline (default params) vs tuned (re-tune per fraction)
          10 seeds per fraction, σ=0

 Phase 5: Noise injection — 3 noise types (Gaussian, systematic bias, gross errors)
          Two arms: baseline vs tuned (re-tune on noisy data, CV inherits noise)
          At full N, training labels only — test always clean

 Phase 5b: Validation quality sub-experiment
           At 3 sizes (full N, 25%, 5%): noisy val vs clean val during tuning

 Phase 6: 2D grid — f(fraction, noise_level) surface, both arms
          Models: LightGBM, RF, MPNN2, BayesianRidge (baseline only)

 Experiment loop (tuned arm):
   tune_lightgbm(X_sub, y_noisy) → evaluate(X_test_clean, y_test_clean)
 Experiment loop (baseline arm):
   LGBMRegressor(**defaults).fit(X_sub, y_noisy) → evaluate(X_test_clean, y_test_clean)
